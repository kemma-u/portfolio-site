About

My Story

Hi, my name is Kemma. I am a fast learner and highly-organized Quality Assurance Analyst-turned-UX Designer. With my first-hand knowledge of an agile environment, I have honed my communication skills allowing me to fully understand a project at all angles. I strive for perfection in all aspects of my life and with my self-taught skills in design applications like Adobe XD, I am prepared for any challenge that comes my way. It is important for me to discover new solutions and make people’s lives easier and more enjoyable by making the world more efficient and fun. I am ready to make a difference in an innovative team that places both their client’s and employees at the forefront of their company mission.

My goal is to become a full-time user experience designer at an altruistic, tech company in the tri-state area where I can be free to be creative as well as have variety in my day. While I am a fast learner, I have an eye for specific details and can bring my varying perspectives to help cultivate numerous ideas.

Outside of design, I enjoy relaxing on the beach, going for hikes, cooking a variety of dishes, traveling the world, and exploring the city. If you share any of my passions or interests, send me a message and let’s discuss how we can help each other.
